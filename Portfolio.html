PAST PROJECTS.

Company's Successful campaigns.

SABRE Award 2024.

       Hotel triangle was the first hotel in Africa to win SABRE Award."Superior Achiement in Branding,Reputation, and Engagement.A prestigious award organised by The Holmes Report. "

Food and Medical Donation.

       ON 24th March 2024 Hotel Triangle held a successful compaign while visiting Nakivale Refugee Settlement and gave out food and medicine to the Refugees.

Cancer Marathon.

       Hotel triangle conducted Cancer Marathon Campaign in Mbarara City where 5 million dollars were collected to help cancer patients at Mbarara Regional Hospital.

World Travel Award 2018.

       Hotel triangle was the first Hotel in Uganda to be awarded WT Award.This is considered the "Oscars ofb the travel industry". It also recognise excellence in various sectors of the tourism, travel, and hospitality industries.

Uganda's Best NGO Aword 2016.

       Hotel triangle was awarded as the best performer among the Non Government Organitions in Uganda in a year 2016.

Suport to the red cross.

       Hotel Triangle also joined Red Cross Organisation in blood donation healthy sensitisation to the students at Mountains Of The Moon University Fortportal.
