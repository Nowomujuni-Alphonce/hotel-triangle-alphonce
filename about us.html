OUR LOCATION

                  Hotel Triangle Mbarara is located in the heart of western Uganda along the Mbarara Masaka Highway. An extremely charming, quiet and lively atmosphere overlooking hilly terrain of Mbarara.On entering this charming hotel, you will immediately sense its special intimate luxurious atmosphere. Each detail has been passionately chosen and each room deserves a visit.The special charm and cozy mood of the hotel Triangle Mbarara will make you feel at home.

                    Find your peace at our wellness Centre after exhausting business meeting and long tour in western Uganda. Our diverse wellness will give you new energy to face the challenges of today. Experience wellness with our program of oriental and Traditional massages, or give your skin a new, beautiful glow with our facial and body treatments. After the massage, to complete your enjoyment, feel free to use our complementary 30 minutes of sauna.
